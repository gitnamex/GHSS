GHSS MANKADA PALLIPPURAM

Established in 1974 and located in Malappuram, Koottilangadi Panchayath, our school occupies 4 acres and 4 cents of land, graciously donated by Narasmana Shankaran Nanmboothirippadu and Vasudevan Namboothirippadu. We provide education for 11th and 12th grades, focusing on comprehensive development and preparing students for future success. With well-maintained classrooms, a library housing a diverse collection of books, separate functional toilets, and a computer-aided learning lab with 15 computers, we offer an ideal learning environment. Our dedicated teachers and supportive staff nurture students' growth. Join us at GHSS MANKADA PALLIPPURAM, where we empower minds and shape futures.

Streams

Explore Different Subject Streams?

Discover diverse academic streams, including science, computer science, commerce, and humanities. Choose your desired stream for a fulfilling educational journey.





Science

Explore the fascinating world of science and discover the wonders of the natural world. From physics and chemistry to biology and astronomy, our science program offers hands-on experiments, engaging lectures, and exciting field trips. Ignite your curiosity and embark on a journey of scientific discovery.




Computer Science

Unleash your passion for technology and dive into the world of computer science. Learn programming languages, develop software applications, and acquire the skills needed for a career in the digital age. From web development to artificial intelligence, our computer science program equips you with the knowledge and expertise to thrive in the tech industry.




Commerce

Step into the world of business and commerce and unlock a realm of opportunities. Our commerce program offers a comprehensive understanding of finance, marketing, accounting, and entrepreneurship. Gain practical skills, learn about market dynamics, and develop a strategic mindset to succeed in the dynamic business landscape.




Humanities

Immerse yourself in the realm of humanities and explore the rich tapestry of human culture, history, and expression. Dive into literature, philosophy, social sciences, and the arts. Discover the power of critical thinking, creativity, and empathy as you delve into the complexities of the human experience.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the admission process at GHSS Mankada Pallippuram?

    The admission process at GHSS Mankada Pallippuram involves submitting an application form, attending an interview, and providing relevant documents. Our admissions team will guide you through the process and assist with any queries you may have.

  • Is transportation available for students?

    Yes, we provide transportation facilities for students residing in designated areas. Our school buses are equipped with safety measures and are operated by experienced drivers. Please contact the school administration for more information about bus routes and fees.

  • Are there any extracurricular activities offered at the school?

    Yes, we offer a range of extracurricular activities to enhance students' overall development. These include sports, arts and crafts, music, dance, and various clubs and societies. Students are encouraged to participate and explore their interests beyond academics.

  • How can parents stay updated about their child's progress?

    We believe in maintaining effective communication with parents. We conduct regular parent-teacher meetings to discuss students' progress. Additionally, we provide online portals or mobile apps where parents can access attendance records, academic reports, and communicate with teachers.

  • Are there any scholarship programs available?

    Yes, GHSS Mankada Pallippuram offers scholarship programs based on merit and financial need. Eligible students can apply for scholarships, and the selection process is carried out by the scholarship committee. Detailed information about scholarships and the application process can be obtained from the school administration.

  • What are the facilities available for students at GHSS Mankada Pallippuram?

    Our school is equipped with modern facilities to provide students with a conducive learning environment. These include well-equipped classrooms, a library with a vast collection of books, science and computer labs, sports facilities, and a cafeteria. We strive to create a comfortable and enriching experience for our students.
